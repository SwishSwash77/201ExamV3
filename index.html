What's going on with the Angels?

It's 2024 and a new season of baseball. What should Angels fans pay attention to this season? Who's performing well and who's underperforming? Do the Angels have a chance at getting to the playoffs? We answer all these questions and more! If you go to our stats page you will learn more about legendary Mike Trout and see his stats.

Shoreline

The past couple seasons for the Angels have been frustrating, to say the least. Injuries and inconsistency plagued the team, leaving their playoff hopes in the dust. But this year feels different. With Mike Trout healthy and back to his MVP form, the offense is clicking. The pitching staff has shown promising signs, and the overall team chemistry seems strong. While there's still a long season ahead, the Angels are finally playing to their potential, and a return to postseason glory feels like a real possibility.

Mike Trout

2024 has been a resurgence year for Mike Trout. After battling injuries the past few seasons, he's come out swinging. Trout's power is on full display, launching numerous towering home runs, already surpassing the 8-homer mark. He's not just a power threat though, showcasing his well-rounded game with a scorching batting average. This hot start has him leading the league in home runs and has Angel fans excited for a potential deep playoff run.

Taylor Ward

Despite ending last season early by getting hit in the face with a fastball last year, Taylor Ward has emerged as a breakout star for the Angels in 2024. Ward is stepping up alongside a resurgent Mike Trout and he’s been a clutch performer. Ward’s delivered multiple walk-off hits, including a dramatic game-winning single in the 9th inning. Ward's power has also been on display with several home runs, including a crucial two-run blast to add insurance in a tight game. His hot hitting and knack for coming up big in key moments are making him a fan favorite and a key contributor to the Angels' early-season success.
