Will Mike Trout be a Hall of Famer?

Mike Trout's enshrinement in the Baseball Hall of Fame is a near certainty. At a young age, he's already amassed a staggering collection of awards, including three MVP titles, nine Silver Slugger Awards, and a historic back-to-back All-Star Game MVP showing. Beyond the hardware, his statistics are simply dominant, with a career batting average over .300 and a Wins Above Replacement (WAR) that ranks him among the best active players and on pace for Hall of Fame status. With continued production, Trout has a legitimate shot at reaching exclusive milestones like 3,000 hits and 600 home runs, solidifying his place as one of baseball's all-time greats.

  1. Batting Percentage

    • During his record-breaking rookie year in 2011, Trout reached a batting average of .344 after 100 games!
    • As of today, April 19th, 2024, Trout's current batting average for the 2024 season is .295 against right-handed pitchers!
    • Trout's highest batting average for a single season was in 2012, when he hit .326!

  2. Home Runs!

    • Mike Trout reached the impressive milestone of 300 career home runs in the shortened 2020 season!
    • Trout's most prolific home run year was 2019, when he crushed a career-high 45 homers!
    • He achieved the feat of joining an elite group by hitting 30 home runs before the age of 27 in five different seasons!

  3. Awards!

    • Won the American League's prestigious Silver Slugger Award a whopping nine times.!
    • American League Rookie of the Year in 2012!
    • Trout won three MLB Most Valuable Player Awards in 2014, 2016, and 2019.!
